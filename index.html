7 Gut-Healing Smoothie Recipes

Boost your gut health with these nourishing and delicious smoothies.

These smoothies are designed to support gut health by balancing the gut microbiome, reducing inflammation, and promoting the growth of beneficial bacteria such as Bifidobacteria and Lactobacillus. If you're experiencing issues like gas, irregular bowel movements, or energy dips after meals, these smoothies can help address potential gut dysbiosis and promote overall digestive health. Polyphenol Power Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 green banana - prebiotic, feeds good bacteria
  • 1 tbsp flaxseeds
  • 1/2 avocado - healthy fats, gut healing
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate juice
  • 1/2 cup kombucha
  • 1 cup coconut water

Why: Rich in polyphenols, prebiotics, and probiotics to target Bacteroides overgrowth and promote good bacteria like Bifidobacteria.

Green Gut Detox Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 green banana - prebiotic, feeds good bacteria
  • 1/2 cup kefir
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 tbsp flaxseeds

  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1 cup almond milk

Why: Boosts beneficial bacteria (Bifidobacteria and Lactobacillus) with prebiotics and probiotics, while reducing gut inflammation.

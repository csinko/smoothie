Boost your gut health with these nourishing and delicious smoothies.

If you're experiencing issues like gas, irregular bowel movements, or energy dips after meals, these smoothies can help address potential gut dysbiosis and promote overall digestive health. Polyphenol Power Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach (Rich in vitamins and minerals, supports overall health)
  • 1/2 cup blueberries (polyphenols, fight inflammation)
  • 1/2 green banana (prebiotic, feeds good bacteria)
  • 1 tbsp flaxseeds (fiber, supports gut bacteria)
  • 1/2 avocado (healthy fats, gut healing)
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate juice (polyphenols, reduce harmful bacteria)
  • 1/2 cup kombucha (probiotics, support gut balance)
  • 1 cup coconut water (hydration)

Macronutrients:

Total Calories: 399.0

Protein: 5.5g (6.4%) Fat: 15.8g (18.4%) Carbs: 64.8g (75.3%)

Why: Rich in polyphenols, prebiotics, and probiotics to target Bacteroides overgrowth and promote good bacteria like Bifidobacteria.
